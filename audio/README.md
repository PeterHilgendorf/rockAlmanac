## The sleep script

Written for an unhurried, even, monotone AI read — slower than average,
about ninety to one hundred and twenty words per minute. At that pace
the spoken portion (~4,300 words) runs roughly **37 to 48 minutes**;
even at a brisk near-normal pace it stays over half an hour, and it
never approaches an hour.

The file opens with a short production note. **Reading begins at the
marked line** — everything above it is for the person setting up the
narration, not for the voice. Numbers are spelled out as words on
purpose, to keep the read slow and even and to avoid misreadings.

All facts come from the verified Rock Almanac dataset; sensitive moments
(illness, deaths) are kept gentle and brief, as the genre calls for.
